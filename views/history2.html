Why It Matters

Climate change matters because it threatens human health, safety, and livelihoods, and negatively impacts all life on Earth.

It leads to more extreme weather events, rising sea levels, and disrupted ecosystems and economies.

It creates escalating challenges for populations, especially in vulnerable regions, making it essential to act now to reduce emissions and build resilience.
