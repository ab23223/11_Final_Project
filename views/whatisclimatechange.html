Climate Change in a Nutshell

Climate change refers to long-term shifts in global temperatures and weather patterns. While it happens naturally, human activities like burning fossil fuels have accelerated it dramatically.

  • 🌡️ Global average temperature has risen by ~1.1°C since the 19th century.
  • 🌊 Sea levels are rising due to melting ice sheets.
  • 🔥 More frequent and severe extreme weather events.

Truths & Myths

🌍 Myth: "Climate change is just natural cycles."
❗ Fact: Scientific evidence shows the current warming trend is primarily driven by human activities, not natural cycles.
❄️ Myth: "If the Earth is warming, why do we still get cold winters?"
❗ Fact: Climate change increases extremes. Local cold spells still occur, but overall global temperatures are rising.
💨 Myth: "CO₂ is only a tiny part of the atmosphere, so it can’t matter."
❗ Fact: Even in small amounts, CO₂ traps heat effectively. Small increases have a big impact on global climate.
